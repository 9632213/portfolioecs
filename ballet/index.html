What is Ballet?

Ballet is a classical style of dance that originated in the Italian Renissance way back in the 15th century. It is very deciplined requiring a lot of strength and poise. It has been majorly influenced by the French and still uses French terminology today. Ballet has turned into a concert for of dance around the world. With ornate costumes, and production ballets have become a favorite world wide.

Ballet Basics

In ballet classes, there is always a warmup at the Barre. After your Barre work, you move to dancing in the Center and across the floor. Girls wear pink tights, black leotard, and pink ballet shoes as a beginner. As you advance, You can be approved to get your Pointe Shoes. As you advance skirts and tutus can be warn for dress rehersals and preformances.
